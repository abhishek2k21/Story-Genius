# Service Level Agreement (SLA)

## Day 52: Delivery Guarantees

---

## Overview

This SLA defines the delivery commitments for paid agency clients.

---

| Plan | Weekly Delivery | Monthly Total |
|------|-----------------|---------------|

**Delivery Days**: Monday-Friday  
**Batch Size**: 10-25 Shorts per batch

## Response Time

| Priority | Issue Type | Response Time |
|----------|------------|---------------|
| P0 | System down | 30 minutes |
| P1 | Batch fails | 2 hours |
| P2 | Quality issue | 24 hours |
| P3 | Feature request | 48 hours |

---

## Success Rate Guarantee

**Minimum 80% success rate per batch**

If success rate falls below 80%:
1. Immediate notification
2. Root cause analysis within 24 hours
3. Credit for failed videos

---

## Weekly Reporting

Every Monday, clients receive:

1. **Delivery Summary**
   - Videos delivered
   - Success rate
   - Average quality score

2. **Top Performers**
   - Best hook of the week
   - Highest scoring videos

3. **Cost Summary**
   - Credits used
   - Remaining quota
