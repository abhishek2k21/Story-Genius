# Pilot Feedback Questions

## Questions to Ask During Pilot

---

### Workflow Fit

1. Does the client/channel abstraction match how you think about your business?
2. Is the batch generation flow intuitive or confusing?
3. What's missing from the current workflow?

### Quality

4. How would you rate the hook quality (1-10)?
5. Do the videos feel "on-brand" for your clients?
6. What % of generated Shorts would you use as-is vs edit further?

### Controls

7. Are the human-in-the-loop controls sufficient?
8. Do you need more approval checkpoints?
9. Is the hook approval flow helpful or annoying?

### Reporting

10. Does the weekly report answer "what did we deliver?"
11. What metrics are missing?
12. Would you send this report to your client?

### Economics

13. At what price point does this make sense for your agency?
14. How many Shorts/month would you need to justify the cost?
15. Would you replace an editor with this, or use it to scale?

### Dealbreakers

16. What would make you NOT use this?
17. What's the minimum quality threshold you need?
18. Any missing features that are absolute requirements?

### Advocacy

19. Would you recommend this to another agency?
20. What would make you want to tell others about this?

---

## How to Collect

- 30-min call at end of pilot
- Quick survey (5 key questions)
- Open-ended email follow-up

---

## Priority Signals

| Response | Signal |
|----------|--------|
| "I'd replace an editor" | Strong PMF |
| "I'd use it to scale" | Good PMF |
| "I'd edit most outputs" | Quality issue |
| "Too expensive" | Pricing issue |
| "Missing X feature" | Feature gap |
