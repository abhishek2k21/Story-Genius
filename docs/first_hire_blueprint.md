# First Hire Blueprint

## Day 73: Design the First Non-Founder Role

---

## Role Decision

**Choose ONE primary role for first hire:**

- [ ] **Ops / Delivery Manager** - Runs daily operations
- [ ] **Client Success Lead** - Owns client relationships
- [ ] **Platform Engineer** - Builds automation

---

## Recommended First Hire: Ops / Delivery Manager

### Why This Role First

1. **Frees founder time** - Daily ops is biggest time sink
2. **Client-facing but structured** - Clear playbook exists
3. **Low risk** - Mistakes are recoverable
4. **High leverage** - Enables scaling to 20+ clients

---

## Role Definition

### Title
**Ops & Delivery Manager**

### Mission
> Keep clients happy through reliable, on-time delivery without founder involvement in daily operations.

### Responsibilities

| Area | Tasks |
|------|-------|
| **Delivery** | Monitor batches, ensure SLA, handle retries |
| **Client Ops** | Onboarding (after sales), weekly reports, support |
| **Quality** | Spot-check outputs, flag issues |
| **Reporting** | Update dashboard, raise risks |

### NOT Responsibilities

- ❌ Pricing decisions
- ❌ Sales / new client acquisition
- ❌ Technical fixes / code changes
- ❌ Strategic planning

---

## Success Metrics

| Metric | Target | Measurement |
|--------|--------|-------------|
| Client SLA met | >95% | Weekly |
| Founder time saved | >10 hrs/week | Weekly |
| Client satisfaction | No complaints | Ongoing |
| Onboarding time | <30 min/client | Per client |
| Issue escalation | <2/week | Weekly |

---

## Failure Signals

| Signal | Action |
|--------|--------|
| SLA miss >2x/month | Performance review |
| Client complaint | Immediate review |
| Founder pulled into daily ops | Role clarification |
| Quality issues missed | Training |
| Burnout/overload | Scope reduction |

---

## Compensation Framework

| Component | Range |
|-----------|-------|
| Base | ₹30,000 - 50,000/month |
| Bonus | Up to 20% based on SLA |
| Equity | 0.25 - 0.5% if early |

---

## Hiring Criteria

### Must Have
- [ ] Ops experience (agency/startup)
- [ ] Comfort with dashboards/data
- [ ] Clear written communication
- [ ] Reliable, consistent work style

### Nice to Have
- [ ] Video/content industry experience
- [ ] Basic technical understanding
- [ ] Client-facing experience

### Red Flags
- ❌ Wants to "build features"
- ❌ Uncomfortable with repetition
- ❌ Needs detailed supervision

---

## 30-60-90 Day Plan

### Day 1-30: Shadow & Learn
- Shadow all client interactions
- Learn platform inside out
- Handle 1-2 clients independently

### Day 31-60: Take Over
- Own daily delivery for all clients
- Founder cc'd but not primary
- Weekly 1:1 reviews

### Day 61-90: Independent
- Full ownership of delivery ops
- Founder only for escalations
- Begin suggesting improvements

---

## Decision

**Hire this role when:**
- [ ] 8+ active clients
- [ ] Founder spending >15 hrs/week on ops
- [ ] Revenue covers 3x salary

**Do NOT hire if:**
- [ ] <5 clients
- [ ] Founder still figuring out process
- [ ] No clear playbook exists
