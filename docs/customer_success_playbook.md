### 3. Pro Users ðŸ’Ž

**Profile**:
- Paying customers
- Creating videos regularly
- Core revenue segment

**Goal**: **Retention & Advocacy**

**Touchpoints**:

| Frequency | Action | Channel | Goal |
|-----------|--------|---------|------|
| Monthly | Check-in email | Email | Ensure satisfaction |
| Quarterly | Success review call | Phone/Video | Build relationship |
| On feature launch | Early access | Email + Product | Show value |
| Ongoing | Referral program | Email + In-app | Drive advocacy |

**Success Metrics**:
- âœ… Monthly churn < 3%
- âœ… NPS > 50
- âœ… Referrals > 0.5 per customer
- âœ… Lifetime > 12 months

**Playbook**:

**Monthly check-in**:
1. Email: "How can we help you succeed?"
2. Track video creation velocity
3. Identify expansion opportunities

**Churn risk indicators**:
- Zero videos in 14 days
- Usage drop > 50%
- Support tickets with negative sentiment
- Failed payment

**When churn risk detected**:
1. Personal outreach from CS team
2. Offer help, training, or resources
3. **Important**: Ask "What would make you stay?"
4. Escalate to retention specialist if needed

**Advocacy program**:
1. Invite to refer friends (dual rewards)
2. Request testimonials/case studies
3. Invite to beta features
4. Feature in marketing materials

### 4. At-Risk Users ðŸš¨

**Profile**:
- Inactive 30+ days
- Previously active
- Potential to win back

**Goal**: **Win-back**

**Touchpoints**:

| Days Inactive | Action | Channel | Goal |
|---------------|--------|---------|------|
| 30 | "We Miss You" email | Email | Re-engage |
| 60 | Feedback survey | Email | Understand why |
| 90 | Special offer | Email | Final attempt |

**Success Metrics**:
- âœ… Win-back rate > 20%
- âœ… Feedback response > 10%

**Playbook**:

**30-day inactive email**:
```
Subject: We miss you! What can we improve?

Hi [Name],

We noticed you haven't created any videos lately.
We'd love to help you get back on track.

What's changed? (reply with one click)
- Too busy
- Technical issues
- Found another tool
- Don't need it anymore

We're here to help!
```

**60-day feedback survey**:
- Send 3-question survey
- Ask: Why stopped? What would bring you back? What can we improve?
- Offer $25 gift card for completion

**90-day special offer**:
- Last-chance email
- 3 months Pro for price of 1
- "We want you back" campaign
- If no response, mark as churned
