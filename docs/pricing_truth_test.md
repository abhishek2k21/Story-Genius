# Pricing Truth Test

## Day 47: Test Willingness to Pay

---

## Rules

1. Present ONE price, ONE limit, ONE outcome
2. Do NOT negotiate
3. Do NOT defend
4. OBSERVE reactions

---

## The Pitch (Script)

"Based on our pilot, here's what we're offering:

**Agency Pilot Plan**
- 500 Shorts per month
- All features included
- Weekly reports
- Priority support

**Price: ₹10,000/month**

This would let you generate ~125 Shorts per week for all your clients."

[STOP. WAIT. OBSERVE.]

---

## Reaction Logging

| Reaction | What It Means | Action |
|----------|---------------|--------|
| Immediate "Yes" | Price low | Consider higher tier |
| "Let me think" | Normal buying | Follow up in 2 days |
| "Too expensive" | Price objection | Probe for value gap |
| "What do I get?" | Value unclear | Clarify ROI |
| Silence | Processing | Wait, don't fill |
| Counter-offer | Engaged, negotiating | Note their price |

---

## Questions to Ask After

1. "What would this need to deliver for that price to feel like a no-brainer?"

2. "If the price was right, would you use this next month?"

3. "What's your current spend on Shorts production?"
