## Customer Tiers

### 1. Free Tier
**Target**: Individual creators, hobbyists  
**Features**: Basic video creation, 5 videos/month  
**Onboarding**: Self-service

### 2. Pro Tier ($29/month)
**Target**: Professional creators, small teams  
**Features**: Unlimited videos, advanced features, priority support  
**Onboarding**: Guided

### 3. Enterprise Tier (Custom pricing)
**Target**: Agencies, large organizations  
**Features**: Custom integrations, SLA, dedicated support  
**Onboarding**: White-glove

## Free Tier Onboarding (Self-Service)

### Step 1: Sign Up
```
URL: https://ytvideocreator.com/signup

Required Fields:
  - Email address
  - Password (min 8 characters)
  - Name
  - Optional: Company name

Process:
  1. User fills signup form
  2. Email verification sent
  3. User clicks verification link
  4. Account activated
```

### Step 2: Welcome Email
**Sent**: Immediately after email verification

**Content**:
```markdown
Subject: Welcome to YT Video Creator! ðŸŽ‰

Hi [Name],

Welcome aboard! Your account is now active.

Quick Start:
1. Create your first video: https://app.ytvideocreator.com/create
2. Watch tutorial (3 min): https://ytvideocreator.com/tutorial
3. Browse templates: https://app.ytvideocreator.com/templates

Need help?
- Documentation: https://docs.ytvideocreator.com
- Community: https://community.ytvideocreator.com
- Support: support@ytvideocreator.com

Happy creating!
The YT Video Creator Team
```

### Step 3: In-App Tutorial
**Triggers**: On first login

**Flow**:
1. Welcome modal with key features
2. Interactive walkthrough:
   - Choose a template
   - Customize content
   - Generate video
   - Download/share
3. Completion: Badge + 1 bonus video credit

### Step 4: First Video Creation
**Goal**: User creates first video within 24 hours

**Success Metrics**:
- Time to first video: < 10 minutes
- Completion rate: > 70%
- User satisfaction: > 4/5 stars

## Pro Tier Onboarding (Guided)

### Step 1: Sales Handoff
**Process**:
1. Sales team closes deal
2. Creates ticket in CRM
3. Tags Customer Success Manager (CSM)
4. CSM reaches out within 24 hours

### Step 2: Welcome Call (15 minutes)
**Scheduled**: Within 48 hours of signup

**Agenda**:
1. Introductions (2 min)
2. Understand use case (5 min)
3. Show key features (5 min)
4. Schedule training (2 min)
5. Q&A (1 min)

**Scripts**:
```markdown
"Hi [Name], welcome to YT Video Creator Pro!

I'm [CSM Name], your dedicated Customer Success Manager. 
I'll be here to help you get the most out of the platform.

First, tell me about your goals. What brings you to YT Video Creator?"

[Listen and take notes]

"Great! Let me show you how our [relevant features] can help with that..."

[Screen share demo]

"How about we schedule a 30-minute training session? When works for you?"

[Schedule training]

"Any questions before we wrap up?"
```

### Step 3: Account Setup
**Completed by**: CSM or user with guidance

**Checklist**:
- [ ] Profile setup (name, company, logo)
- [ ] Team members invited (if applicable)
- [ ] Integrations connected (YouTube, Dropbox, etc.)
- [ ] Billing information verified
- [ ] Workspace created
- [ ] First project created

**Tools**: Setup wizard in app

### Step 4: Training Session (30 minutes)
**Scheduled**: Within 1 week of signup

**Agenda**:
1. Platform overview (5 min)
2. Video creation workflow (10 min)
3. Advanced features (10 min)
4. Templates and customization (5 min)

**Deliverables**:
- Recording of session
- Quick reference guide (PDF)
- Tips & tricks document

### Step 5: Check-in After 1 Week
**Format**: Email + optional call

**Email Template**:
```markdown
Subject: How's it going with YT Video Creator?

Hi [Name],

It's been a week since we got you started. How's everything going?

Quick check:
- Videos created so far: [X]
- Any blockers or questions?
- Features you'd like to learn more about?

I'm here to help! Reply to this email or schedule a quick call: [Calendar link]

Best,
[CSM Name]
```

## Enterprise Tier Onboarding (White-Glove)

### Step 1: Kickoff Meeting (1 hour)
**Attendees**:
- Customer: Executive sponsor, IT lead, end users
- Us: Account Executive, CSM, Solutions Engineer, Product Manager (if needed)

**Agenda**:
1. Introductions & roles (10 min)
2. Business objectives & success criteria (15 min)
3. Technical requirements (15 min)
4. Implementation plan (15 min)
5. Timeline & next steps (5 min)

**Deliverables**:
- Meeting notes
- Implementation plan document
- RACI matrix

### Step 2: Requirements Gathering
**Process**:
1. Detailed discovery calls
2. Document requirements (functional & technical)
3. Review infrastructure (SSO, network, compliance)
4. Define integrations needed

**Template**: `docs/enterprise_requirements.md`

### Step 3: Custom Setup
**Activities**:
- SSO/SAML configuration
- Custom integrations (APIs, webhooks)
- Dedicated infrastructure (if needed)
- Custom branding
- Compliance validation (SOC 2 audit report sharing)

**Timeline**: 2-4 weeks

### Step 4: Training Sessions (Multiple)
**Session 1: Admin Training (2 hours)**
- Platform administration
- User management
- Security settings
- Analytics & reporting

**Session 2: End User Training (1 hour)**
- Video creation basics
- Templates and workflows
- Best practices

**Session 3: Advanced Training (1 hour)** (Optional)
- API usage
- Advanced integrations
- Custom workflows

**Format**: Live + recorded

### Step 5: Dedicated Success Manager
**Responsibilities**:
- Weekly check-in calls (first month)
- Bi-weekly calls (months 2-3)
- Monthly business reviews (ongoing)
- Escalation point for issues
- Feature requests coordination

**Communication**:
- Dedicated Slack channel
- Email: [customername]@ytvideocreator.com
- Phone/video: Scheduled calls

### Step 6: Quarterly Business Reviews
**Format**: Executive presentation

**Content**:
- Usage metrics
- ROI analysis
- Feature adoption
- Upcoming roadmap
- Success stories
